About Me

Hello! I'm Brady Wenzbauer, a passionate graphic designer. My journey in design started with a love of creating art. Over time, I have developed skills in digital graphics, photography, and model-making, which allow me to bring unique perspectives to every project I tackle.

I studied at Davis Technical College and Weber State University, where I honed my technical abilities and discovered the power of design in communicating ideas effectively. Combining my attention to detail with creativity, I strive to craft designs that not only look great but also connect with the audience.

My skills include Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, HTML/CSS, responsive web design, branding, and Apple Logic Pro.

Beyond design, I'm passionate about photography, fine-detailed model-making, and learning new techniques to improve my craft. I value problem-solving, teamwork, and continuous growth, and I bring dedication and curiosity to every project I undertake.
