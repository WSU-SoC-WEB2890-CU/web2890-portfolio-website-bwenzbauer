Graphic Art 3

Third project in digital art series at Davis Technical College emphasizing visual storytelling.

Process

Created abstract compositions using color theory and dynamic layering techniques.

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Accessibility Statement

This project includes foundational accessibility elements such as semantic HTML structure and descriptive alt text for images. However, due to its abstract and visually expressive nature, some of the intended meaning may not be fully accessible to users who rely on non-visual interfaces. Future improvements could include expanded written descriptions of visual storytelling elements, improved contrast and readability considerations, and further accessibility testing with assistive technologies.
