Phone Screensaver

Created as a Davis Technical College project, focusing on aesthetic mobile designs.

Process

Designed visually pleasing graphics that fit mobile screen ratios and resolutions.

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Accessibility Statement

This project includes basic accessibility features such as semantic HTML structure and descriptive alt text for images. However, as a visually driven mobile design, the content relies heavily on color, layout, and imagery, which may not be fully accessible to users with visual impairments. Future improvements could include more detailed text-based descriptions of the design, improved contrast evaluation, and additional testing for accessibility on different devices and assistive technologies.
