Movie Poster

Created as a Davis Technical College project focusing on cinematic poster design.

Process

Selected typography and imagery that conveys the mood and theme of the movie.

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Accessibility Statement

This project includes basic accessibility considerations such as semantic HTML structure and descriptive alt text for images. However, as a cinematic poster design, it relies heavily on visual composition, typography, and imagery to communicate meaning, which may not fully translate to non-visual users. Future improvements could include more detailed written descriptions of visual themes, improved contrast analysis, and additional accessibility testing with assistive technologies.
