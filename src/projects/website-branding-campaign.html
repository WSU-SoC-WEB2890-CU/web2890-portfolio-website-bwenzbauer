Tools Used

Accessibility Statement

This project includes foundational accessibility support such as semantic HTML structure and descriptive alt text for images. However, as a branding campaign focused on visual identity, many elements rely on color, layout, and imagery that may not fully translate to users with visual impairments. Future improvements could include expanded text-based explanations of branding assets, improved contrast and readability checks, and additional accessibility testing with assistive technologies.
