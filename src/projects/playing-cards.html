Playing Cards

Designed a custom set of playing cards for a client project, focusing on unique artwork and visual clarity.

Process

Created custom illustrations for each card and ensured designs were balanced and readable.

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Accessibility Statement

This project includes foundational accessibility considerations such as semantic HTML structure and descriptive alt text for images. However, because the design is highly visual and relies on fine details and color-coded elements, some content may not be fully accessible to users with visual impairments. Future improvements could include clearer text-based descriptions of each card design, stronger contrast validation, and additional accessibility testing with assistive technologies to improve usability.
