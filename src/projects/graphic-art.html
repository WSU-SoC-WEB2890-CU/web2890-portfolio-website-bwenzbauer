Accessibility Statement

This project includes basic accessibility considerations such as semantic HTML structure and descriptive alt text for images. However, the design is primarily visual and exploratory, which may limit accessibility for users relying on assistive technologies. Future improvements could include more detailed written descriptions of visual compositions, enhanced contrast checks, and further accessibility testing to improve usability across different devices and assistive tools.
